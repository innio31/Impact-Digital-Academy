Our Journey

Impact Digital was founded with a clear vision: to create meaningful impact through technology. What began as a small initiative to provide digital skills training has grown into a comprehensive organization offering both digital solutions and professional education.

Recognizing the growing digital divide in our communities, we expanded our mission to include not just education but also practical digital solutions for businesses and institutions. Today, we operate through two interconnected pillars: Impact Digital Services and Impact Digital Academy.

Our approach combines technical expertise with a deep understanding of local challenges. We believe that true digital transformation happens when technology meets human potential.

2021

Foundation

Started as a digital skills training initiative, focusing on basic computer literacy and programming.

2023

Expansion

Launched digital services division to provide IT solutions to businesses and educational institutions.

2024

Dual Pillars

Formally established two divisions: Impact Digital Services and Impact Digital Academy.

2025

Growth

Served 500+ projects and trained 1000+ students across Nigeria, with plans for national expansion.
