Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our services and academy

What services does Impact Digital offer?

We offer comprehensive digital solutions including CBT setup (online/offline), IT procurement, custom software development, company registration (CAC), IT consultancy, and system integration services.

How do I enroll in training programs?

You can enroll through our student portal (impactdigitalacademy.com.ng/portals), call our admissions line (+234 903 544 8295), or visit our office in Ota. We offer both online and in-person training options.

What is the response time for inquiries?

We respond to WhatsApp messages within minutes, phone calls are answered during business hours, and emails are responded to within 24 hours. Urgent matters receive priority attention.

Do you offer corporate training packages?

Yes! We provide customized corporate training packages for organizations. Contact our academy department for tailored programs, group discounts, and on-site training options.

How long does company registration take?

Company registration typically takes less than a week depending on CAC processing times. We handle the entire process from name reservation to certificate collection and post-registration compliance.
