What is Touch Typing?

üè† 2. The Home Row Position

Understanding the Home Row

A S D F ¬†¬†¬† J K L ;

Left hand: A(pinky) S(ring) D(middle) F(index) ‚Äî Right hand: J(index) K(middle) L(ring) ;(pinky)

bumps F and J have raised indicators to find home row without looking.

Hand and Wrist Posture

  • Wrists floating, straight, not resting on desk while typing
  • Fingers curved gently, parallel to keyboard slope

Common Home Row Mistakes

Mistake Correction
Resting wrists on desk Float wrists, use armrests only for arms
Hands too far left/right Align body with B key
Looking at hands Use cloth or blank keyboard

üó∫Ô∏è 4. Keyboard Geography

Five Rows & Modifier Keys

  • Number row: 1 2 3 4 5 6 7 8 9 0 - =
  • Top row: Q W E R T Y U I O P [ ] \
  • Home row: A S D F G H J K L ; '
  • Bottom row: Z X C V B N M , . /

Shift / Enter / Backspace: opposite pinky for Shift; right pinky for Enter/Backspace.
